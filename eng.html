Research Interest

Currently, I am a Ph.D. candidate of National Engineering Research Center for E-Learning (NERCEL) at Faculty of Artifical Intelligence in Education, Central China Normal University. My research interests include knowledge graph representation learning and its applications, temporal knowledge graph and deep learning (convolutional neural networks, graph neural networks, etc.).
